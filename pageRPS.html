Rock Paper Scissors


In this HTML, CSS, and JavaScript project,
I designed a rock-paper-scissors game where users can play against the computer.
The computer's responses are randomized for added challenge.
Upon completing the game, the user's score is stored in the local storage,
creating a personalized and persistent gaming experience.
The project seamlessly blends front-end technologies to deliver
an interactive and enjoyable gaming interface.
